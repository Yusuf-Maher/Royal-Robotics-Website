What Is FIRST Robotics?
FIRST Robotics, is a worldwide organization that provides students who are passionate in, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through hands-on robotics competitions.

These competitions bring students from different schools and age groups to design, build, and program robots to compete in various tasks and challenges.

These competitions not only teach valuable technical skills but also promote teamwork, as each team has to work together to create a robot. First Robotics provides an amazing learning experience that is fun and competitive.
